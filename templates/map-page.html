Represent Bihar

Bihar

Stories are the embodiment of our unique experiences and journeys, provide the narrative behind our myths, legends and folktales, and are our primary way of remembering the past. Represent provides a platform for the sharing of stories of all types, from all places and people. Our first collection of stories is from the state of Bihar. Through a variety of dialects and modalities, they tell us about the culture and history of Bihar using stories and songs. We hope you enjoy the opportunity to explore this ancient land.
